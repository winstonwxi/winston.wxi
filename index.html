Reading Notes

From The Timeless Way of Building by Christopher Alexander: "There is one timeless way of building. It is a thousand years old, and the same today as it has ever been."

He's talking about architecture, but he might as well be talking about anything worth making. The principles don't change. Clarity, proportion, honesty. Whether you're building a house or a website.
